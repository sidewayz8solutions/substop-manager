FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Is SubStop really free?

Yes. SubStop is 100% free with no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no ads. We believe everyone deserves to know where their money is going.

How does my data stay private?

All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We don't have servers, we don't collect analytics, and we don't sell data. Period.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can export your subscription list as JSON or CSV at any time. Your data belongs to you.

What platforms is SubStop available on?

SubStop is available on iOS and Android. A web version is coming soon. Your data can be synced between devices via encrypted backup files.

How do the cancellation links work?

We maintain a database of direct cancellation URLs for 500+ popular services. When you tap "Cancel" on a subscription, we take you straight to the cancellation page — no more hunting through 5 layers of settings menus.
